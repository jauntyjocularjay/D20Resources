### Tactical Military (TACMIL) Sniper Rifle
With the success of the OICW assault rifle, a sniper rifle equivalent was developed. The rifle itself fires a standard 7.62mm round and sports a rangefinding scope (the scope, rangefinding laser gadget) that can connect to a Land Warrior combat armor computer. Additionally, the TacMil sniper rifle features a flash suppressor as well as an effective sound suppressor, ensuring maximum stealth.

### Twin Thunder Machine Gun
The Twin Thunder is a reduced-recoil .50 caliber machine gun capable of dispensing roughly 260 rounds per minute. The machine gun is designed for use in combat by two soldiers, a single gunner and an aide to feed the ammunition belt into the weapon. Its reduced recoil allows for greater accuracy over longer distances with less variance in the aim of the user.

### Grenades, Missiles, and Mines
Most explosives are given in the form of grenades. These grenades provide the base form for these weapons, but equivalent missiles or mines can also exist. To create a missile from a grenade covered here, simply take the base statistics of the grenade and use them as the statistics for ammunition for a missile launcher. Be sure to adjust the range increment, since missiles travel much farther than thrown grenades. Mines are treated in almost the opposite way; their range increment is eliminated. A mine must be placed to be effective. Despite differences in their form, missiles, mines, and grenades all have the same primary effect regardless of the method of delivery for their payloads.

#### Ammunition, Bio-Agent
Bio-agent ammunition contains a miniscule amount of a biological weapon inside an insulated chamber within each round. Use of bio-agent ammunition causes a –1 penalty to damage. Any living creature damaged by the round is injected with a poisonous bio-agent with effects identical to cyanogen gas.

#### Grenade, Concussion
Concussion grenades deal 4d6 points of nonlethal concussion damage in a 10-foot burst radius, or half damage on a successful Reflex save (DC 15). Robots and other nonliving creatures are unaffected.

#### Grenade, EMP
An EMP grenade releases an electromagnetic pulse that instantly shorts out all electronic devices (including computers) within a 20-foot burst radius. Devices remain nonfunctional until repaired. The EMP grenade deals no damage to living creatures, but a creature with cybernetic attachments takes 1d6 points of electricity damage per attachment (max 5d6) and must succeed on a Fortitude save (DC 15) or be stunned for 1d4 rounds. On a natural 1, each attachment takes 1d6 electricity damage. An EMP grenade deals 5d6 points of electricity damage to a mecha or robot.

#### Grenade, Fireflush
A fireflush grenade contains a chemical compound that combusts immediately upon contact with oxygen. When detonated, it produces geysers of flame in a 10-foot burst radius. All creatures and objects within take 3d6 fire damage and ignite flammable items.

#### Grenade, Tangler
The tangler grenade operates on the same principle as the tangler gun, but targets an area. Any character struck suffers a cumulative –2 penalty to Dexterity until the compound is dissolved. If the penalty equals or exceeds Dexterity, the character is immobilized. Penalties stack with those from tangler guns. Compound dissolves naturally after 8 hours or can be removed by solvaway or similar chemicals.

#### High Frequency Sword
A durable metal alloy sword with microscopic machines in the blade that vibrate at high speeds, giving extra cutting power. The wielder is shielded from vibration by a soft hilt.

#### Laser Weapons
Laser weapons come in many shapes and sizes, from pistols to rifles. They do not use ammunition, but are powered by power packs.

#### Mini-Grenade Launcher
A reduced-size grenade launcher, roughly the size of a large pistol. Uses smaller but potent ammunition.

#### Stun Baton
A long rod with a metal cap that emits a pulse of stunning energy. Any creature hit takes 1d6 bludgeoning damage and must succeed on a Fortitude save (DC 10 + damage dealt) or be stunned for 1d4 rounds.

#### Tangler Gun
Fires condensed balls of sticky compound that immobilize targets. Each hit inflicts a cumulative –2 penalty to Dexterity until dissolved. If the penalty equals or exceeds Dexterity, the target is immobilized. Penalties stack with those from tangler grenades. Compound dissolves naturally after 8 hours or can be removed by solvaway or similar chemicals.

#### Light Combat Armor
Provides less protection than heavier types but allows increased mobility. Consists of reinforced blast vest, pads, and sometimes helmet/visor.

#### Scout Armor
Similar to light combat armor but painted with camouflage patterns (woodland, desert, winter, urban, black). Grants +2 Hide checks in appropriate terrain. Lacks some lower body protection for increased mobility. Often incorporates survival gadgets.

#### Survival Suit
A wetsuit-like suit with pads, recycles water and body waste, provides heat/cooling, and can feed the wearer for up to six days. Absorbs solar energy and stores it in heat cells for warmth, with chemical pouches for backup heat.

#### Aquaconverter
A backpack device with a breather tube, separates water into hydrogen and oxygen, allowing limitless underwater breathing.

#### Chemical, Antitox
A hypodermic injection that analyzes blood and generates an antidote for poisons, curing all effects in 1d6 rounds.

#### Chemical, Boost
A temporary adrenaline-enhancer. Grants +4 Strength, +2 Reflex saves, +10 ft. speed, +4 massive damage threshold for 1 minute. Addictive, with side effects: 10% chance of –2 Dexterity and –1 Fortitude saves for 24 hours. Penalties stack and duration increases with repeated use.

#### Chemical, Neutrad
A hypodermic injection that neutralizes radiation poisoning, curing all effects in 1d4 hours.

#### Chemical, Solvaway
A spray-on chemical that dissolves the compound from tangler guns and grenades, freeing immobilized characters instantly.

#### Chemical, Sporekill
A hypodermic injection that neutralizes most diseases, curing all effects in 1d10 hours. Some engineered diseases are immune.

#### Computer, Card
A credit card-sized computer that functions as a standard computer and can be hooked into any interface or display.

#### Disguise Kit, Morphic
A kit with contact lenses, hair prosthetic, paint-on LCD injector, and vocoder. Grants +6 Disguise checks.

#### Display Contacts
Contact lenses that project a semitransparent image, used for computer displays or as part of a HUD.

#### Medkit, Advanced
Combines first aid, medical, and surgery kits. Grants +2 Treat Injury checks.

#### Medkit, Fast-Use
Functions as first aid and medical kit. Specialized computers and sensors allow Treat Injury as a move action. Cannot be used as a surgery kit.

#### Flash-Seal
A block of metal that, when activated, melts and then solidifies to weld a door shut. The door must be destroyed or cut through to open.

#### Fusion Torch
A blowtorch-like device that produces plasma, dealing 3d10 damage per round to immobile objects. As an improvised weapon, deals 1d10 damage.

#### Intellipicks
A box of tiny machines that can pick almost any conventional lock, granting +20 Disable Device. Cannot open electronic or computerized locks.

#### Jetpack
A backpack and fuel cells for powered flight (60 ft., good maneuverability) for up to 2 hours. Replacement fuel cells: Purchase DC 10.

#### Neural Scrambler
A restraint device placed over the head, interrupts brainwave activity. The wearer cannot act but can move under command. No memory of events while restrained.

#### Plastic Surgery Kit, Personal
A one-use mask and computer that permanently changes facial appearance, including eye and hair color. Takes one hour to use.

#### Sensor, Chemicomp
A handheld computer or gauntlet for finding chemical compounds. Grants +10 Search checks for chemicals.

#### Sensor, Geocomp
A handheld computer or gauntlet for finding minerals. Grants +10 Search checks for minerals.

#### Sensor, Motion
Detects and plots motion within 100 feet, even through walls and solid surfaces.

#### Universal Communicator (Unicom)
An all-in-one communications device for direct or group transmissions, with data port for computer links. Later models include hologram recorder/projector.

#### Violet Rations
Pill-form meals that dissolve under the tongue, providing all nutrients and vitamins for a single meal.

#### Ammunition, Deflecting
Creates a gravity deflection field, causing bullets to bounce off walls. Grants the benefit of the Skip Shot feat.

#### Ammunition, Plasma-Coated
Armor-piercing rounds enveloped in plasma. Reduces armor bonus by 2 (min +1). Half damage is fire, half ballistic.

#### Ammunition, Seeker
Contains a microscopic computer and gravity-bending generators. Grants +1 bonus on all ranged attacks.

#### Concussion Rifle
Fires an artificial gravity pulse that hurls targets. Deals 2d10 concussion damage; target is thrown back 5 ft. per 5 damage. Fortitude save (DC = damage dealt) or knocked prone. Colliding with objects causes fall damage.

#### Concussion Rod
A mace with a gravity generator in the head. Deals 2d8 damage (half bludgeoning, half concussive).

#### Gravity Snare
Resembles a laser rifle with prongs. Fires a ring of gravity energy. Target must Reflex save (DC 13) or be entangled for 1d6 rounds. Escape Artist DC 40 or Strength DC 30 to break free.

#### Grenade, Dissolver
Releases a gas cloud that dissolves organic matter, dealing 2d6 acid damage per round in a 5-ft. burst for 1 minute. Gravity well prevents dispersal.

#### Grenade, Gravitic
Releases gravitational forces, crushing all in a 10-ft. burst for 6d6 bludgeoning damage (Reflex DC 15 half).

#### Grenade, Stun
No damage, but creatures in 15-ft. burst must Reflex save (DC 18) or Fortitude save (DC 15) or be stunned for 1d4 rounds.

#### Plasma Weapons
Condense electrically charged gas into destructive force. Powered by power packs.

#### Rail Gun
Uses gravity pulses to propel metal shards at high velocity.

#### Ammunition, Phasing
Phasing bullets contain teleporters and computers. Negate cover benefits.

#### Beam Sword
A handle generates a solid beam of plasma contained by a gravity-induced force field. The most potent melee weapon of the Energy Age.

#### Cryonic Rifle
Fires a cryonic beam for 3d6 cold damage. Target reduced to negative HP is stabilized; at –10 or fewer HP, set to –9 and stabilized (not dead). Powered by special power packs (10 shots each).

#### Disintegrator
Fires a micro-singularity that obliterates molecular structure. Living creatures reduced to –10 HP are disintegrated. Objects/robots at 0 HP are disintegrated. Powered by special power packs (10 shots each).

#### Grenade, Cryonic
Deals 8d6 cold damage in a 10-ft. burst (Reflex DC 15 half). Freezes and stabilizes creatures reduced to negative HP; at –10 or fewer HP, set to –9 and stabilized.

#### Grenade, Psionic
Affects only psionic creatures. Reflex DC 18 or lose psionic abilities for 1d4 rounds; Will DC 15 or take 1d6 temporary Int, Wis, Cha damage.

#### Grenade, Singularity
Creates a miniature black hole. 5-ft. burst, 15d6 damage (Reflex DC 15 half). Reduces creatures to –10 HP with no trace.

#### Lightning Gun
A bulky weapon that fires a ray of electricity, capable of sweeping large areas quickly. Grants the benefits of the Strafe feat. Powered by special power packs (30 shots each).

#### Pulse Rifle
A fully automatic laser assault rifle, standard issue for heavy assault soldiers. Powered by power packs.

#### Sonic Beam
Fires a pulse of destructive sound (2d6 sonic). Any creature damaged must make a Fortitude save (DC 15) or be deafened and shaken for 1d4 rounds. Powered by power packs.

#### Chemical, Plastiflesh
Contained in a small spray can, plastiflesh bonds with human skin on contact and accelerates healing by providing a layer of artificial skin. Instantly heals 1d4 hit points. No Treat Injury check required.

#### Chemical, Truthtell
A chemical that targets brain areas responsible for lying. Injected subjects must make a Will save (DC 18) or be compelled to speak truthfully for 3d10 minutes. Subjects are aware of the effect and may refuse to answer questions.

#### Energy Shield
A defense mechanism granting resistance 5 to cold, electricity, fire, or sonic/concussion (chosen on activation, can be changed as a move action).

#### Force Field, Personal
Projects a defensive bubble around the user. Two varieties: DR 5/– and DR 10/–. Power pack lasts 2 hours (8 with power backpack).

#### Hologram Player
A disc with light projectors that displays 3D images, from 3 inches to Medium-size. Can be linked to a hologram recorder or unicom for real-time comms.

#### Hologram Recorder
A pen-sized device that records up to 1 hour of 3D images, playable on a hologram player or transferable to a computer. Can be used for real-time holographic comms.

#### Hologuise
A headband that projects a 3D image over the face, granting +10 Disguise checks. Used for disguise or fashion.

#### Matter Shield
Grants DR 5/– against ballistic, bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing attacks. Originally designed for miners, now used by military and law enforcement.

#### Micro-Aural Communicator (Microcom)
Consists of an earpiece, lip node, and wristband for silent communication. Cannot be detected by normal Listen checks.

#### Neural Computer Link
Direct brain-to-computer interface. Allows thought-based commands and instant data transfer. Reduces Computer Use/Research checks to free actions when connected. Can be linked to shepherd chip or nanites for instant identity transfer or piloting.

#### Neural Network Jack, Wireless
Plugs into neural computer link for remote network access. With satellite datalink, connects to any network. Requires neural computer link.

#### Neural Recorder
A cap that collects and stores up to 2 hours of visual/auditory memories. Requires Computer Use check (DC 15) to operate. Unwilling subjects may make a Will save (DC 17) to resist.

#### Photon Shield
Bends light to render the user invisible. Grants +40 Hide (still), +20 Hide (moving), and total concealment (50% miss chance).

#### Polyvox
Translates the languages of different cultures and species.

#### Regen Wand
A device that emits energy waves to promote healing. Used as a first aid or medical kit. Grants +4 Treat Injury checks.

#### Sensor, Robocomp
A hand-held computer or gauntlet for evaluating and repairing robots. Identifies current/max HP and grants +4 Repair checks for robots.
